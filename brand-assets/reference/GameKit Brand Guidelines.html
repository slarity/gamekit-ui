01 / IDENTITY

The mark — 👾

The space-invader is the whole brand. It is the favicon, the avatar, the loading sprite, and the first character of the wordmark. Two forms exist: the emoji glyph (platform-native, used inline and as the avatar) and the pixel sprite (our 11×8 CSS-drawn invader, used at large sizes and in motion).

👾
Emoji glyph + 11×8 pixel sprite — same silhouette, two renderings.

It marches

In motion the sprite cycles two frames (legs alternate) — the classic attract-mode shuffle. Use for loaders and empty states.

It recolors

The sprite is drawn with currentColor. Tint it with any palette accent — never with an off-palette hue.

It stays pixel

Hard edges only. No anti-aliasing, no smoothing, no gradients inside the silhouette. Pixels are the point.

05 / COLOR

Color system

CRT-dark by default: a blue-black stack of surfaces, near-white ink, and a phosphor accent. Green is primary; magenta, cyan, and amber are the secondary CRT triad. All values are authored in oklch so they stay perceptually even.

08 / VOICE

Voice & tone

We talk like a developer who loves arcades — precise about the tech, playful about the games. Short. Confident. A little 8-bit.

A

Dev-honest

Lead with the real numbers: ≤6 KB, zero deps, one file. No hand-waving.

B

Arcade-warm

“Insert coin”, “high score”, “game over”. Borrow the cabinet, not the cheese.

C

Tight

One idea per line. If a sentence can lose words, it loses them.

▸ Say

  • “Drop-in arcade for your shadcn app.”
  • “Give your 404 a high score.”
  • “≤6 KB. Zero deps. One file.”
  • “Reads your tokens — change --primary and the playfield recolors.”

× Don't

  • “Revolutionary gaming framework.”
  • “Leverage synergistic play experiences.”
  • “The #1 best games library ever!!!”
  • “Powered by cutting-edge AI.”
